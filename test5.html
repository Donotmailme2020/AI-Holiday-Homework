Uses of AI

1. AI in E-Commerce

Personalized Shopping: Artificial Intelligence technology is used to create recommendation
engines through which you can engage better with your customers.

AI-powered Assistants: Virtual shopping assistants and chatbots help improve the user
experience while shopping online.


Fraud Prevention: Credit card frauds and fake reviews are two of the most significant issues that
E-Commerce companies deal with.

2. AI in Navigation


Based on research from MIT, GPS technology can provide users with accurate, timely,
and detailed information to improve safety.
