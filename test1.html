About AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is intelligence demonstrated by machines,
unlike the natural intelligence displayed by humans and animals,
which involves consciousness and emotionality.

It's is a wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned with
building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically
require human intelligence. AI is an interdisciplinary science
with multiple approaches, but advancements in machine learning.
