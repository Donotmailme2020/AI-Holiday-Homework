Dominions of AI

Human-Machines interactions
Human-machine interactions refer to the contact between humans and machines to fulfil a specific task or
operation.In AI human-machine interactions done through the following domains:
‣Data
‣Computer Vision (CV)
‣Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Data
If a person thinks of automate any system or want a report or analysis of customers’ feedback, data is
required. Data can be the backbone of AI.Almost 98% of AI systems are dependent on data.

Computer Vision (CV)
It is a field of science that deals with how computers gain a high level of understanding from digital
images or videos. The computer vision includes the following methods to produce information:
‣Acquiring Images
‣Processing Images
‣Analysing Images
‣Understanding Images

Natural Language Processing (NLP)
The programming languages work on their own principles, syntax, and keywords. The aim of NLP is developing
such systems that work on human natural language on oral as well-spoken language.
